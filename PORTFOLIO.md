# Portfolio Brief

## LoyaltyBoost AI

LoyaltyBoost AI is a CRM promotion workspace for loyalty teams. It turns a campaign goal into structured loyalty mechanics, validates launch readiness, previews a wallet-pass customer experience, and exports CRM-ready activation payloads.

## Highlights

- Built a responsive React/Vite product interface for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Added a campaign workspace with saved drafts, lifecycle status, editable launch details, and export actions.
- Replaced browser-stored AI credentials with a Vercel serverless API boundary.
- Added deterministic fallback generation so the product remains usable without live AI configuration.
- Implemented production-style guardrails for margin exposure, redemption friction, fatigue risk, urgency, legal copy, and channel readiness.
- Added wallet QA with barcode preview and Liquid-style customer personalization.
- Added CRM and wallet export JSON for activation handoff.

## Product Thinking

The project is designed for CRM managers and loyalty marketers who need to move from idea to launch-ready campaign without manually juggling reward mechanics, customer copy, wallet previews, and operational QA. The current MVP focuses on making campaign structure, risks, and activation handoff visible before teams spend promotion budget.

## Next Step

The next production step is hosted multi-user persistence with organization accounts, approval workflows, and real CRM/wallet integrations.
